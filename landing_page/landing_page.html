  • our mission statement

    To champion and promote effective and efficient ICT to enable public service delivery and access to information to citizens of Migori county.

  • our vision

    To become a centre of excellence for ICT to promoting e-Governance and transforming lives through innovation.

  • our goals

    1. Provide technology services, systems and support to all staff.
    2. Improve and maintain ICT infrastructure within the county facilities and departmental units.
    3. Manage ICT use and security issues in a comprehensive and coordinated way.
    4. To provide user support and ICT Equipment Maintenance.
    5. Develop Business Continuity (Data backups and Disaster recovery) plans for the county.
    6. Design a program to uplift the ICT human resource capacity to strategic level.
    7. Enforce and implement e-Government standards and and procedures.
    8. Improve interactions and to promote efficient and effective service delivery to the public.
    9. Leverage on ICT to foster innovation for business processes.
    10. Coordinates online service delivery through use of emerging technologies.
